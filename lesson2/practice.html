the national assembly

the national assembly comprises of two hallowed chambers as follows ;

the senate

the house of representatives

both chambers have the responsibility to make laws and also review them, but the senate is however the upper/higher house

leadership of the national assembly

both chambers basically shares the same pettern of leadership structure as follows :

the senate leadership

this in their order of hierrachy consists of ;

  1. the senate president
  2. the deputy senate president
  3. the clerk of the house
  4. the chief whip of the house

the house of reps leadership

this in their order of hierrachy consists of ;

  1. the speaker of the house
  2. the deputy speaker
  3. the clerk of the house
  4. the chief whip

other important positions includes

  • the majority leader
  • the minority leaders
  • chairman of house committees
